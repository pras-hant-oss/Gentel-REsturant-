Our Story

A Local Institution with Heart

Nestled in the historic town of Athlone, Gertie Brownes Bar & Bistro has been a cornerstone of the local community for over a decade. Named after a beloved local figure, our gastropub blends the warmth of a traditional Irish pub with the sophistication of modern bistro dining.

Our kitchen team sources the finest local ingredients — from Hereford beef to fresh Atlantic seafood — crafting dishes that honour Irish culinary heritage while embracing contemporary flavours.
